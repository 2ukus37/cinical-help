About HealthCheck AI

How this tool works and what it can (and can't) do

psychology

How the AI Works

HealthCheck AI uses three machine learning models working together — Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost — trained on real clinical datasets. The models are calibrated to give accurate probability scores, not just yes/no answers.

When you upload a document, our AI reads it and extracts your health values automatically using the stepfun/step-3.5-flash language model.

Your Privacy

Your uploaded documents are processed in memory and never permanently stored. Health check results are saved locally with a random reference ID — no personal information is collected or shared.
